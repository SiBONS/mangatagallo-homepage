Our Maker

Mariana

Trained in diamond cutting and metal smithing, Mariana opened Mangata & Gallo in her hometown of Austin and has designed every piece the house has made since — first alone at a single workbench, now alongside a small team of artisans who help bring each design to life.

“A piece of jewelry should look like it was always going to exist — ours just happened to find it first.”
